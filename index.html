What we do

The goal of our trade tracker app is to turn you into a profitable trader. The key to do this, is risk management, and our application will teach you this.

Trade journaling

Keep track of all your trades in a web-based journal hosted in the cloud.

Performance analytics

Keeps track of trading related key performance indicators (KPI's) to help you improve your trading.

Focus on risk & teaches risk management

Continuously keeps track of how much risk you are taking to help you become profitable.

Integrated with major exchanges

Automate everything by connecting to your favorite exchange.

Automated algo orders
